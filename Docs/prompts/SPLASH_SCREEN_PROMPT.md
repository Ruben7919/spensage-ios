# MichiFinanzas Splash Screen Prompt

Last updated: 2026-04-04

## Goal

Create a cute, polished splash illustration for the in-app startup experience. Unlike the icon, the splash should use the three mascots together.

## Primary Prompt

```text
Create a premium splash screen illustration for MichiFinanzas, a cute but powerful mobile savings app. Show the three mascot characters together: Tikki, Ludo, and Manchas. They should appear as a friendly team inside a soft, magical savings world with a bright teal, mint, cream, and soft gold palette. The background should feel warm, airy, and playful, with subtle glow, floating sparkles, and rounded decorative shapes. Include light savings cues such as coins, a piggy bank, wallet charm, yarn balls, or soft mission-board style props, but keep the composition uncluttered and mobile-first. The tone should feel adorable, polished, and trustworthy, not childish or chaotic. This is for a startup splash, so the scene should have strong central focus, clear silhouette, and room for a logo or loading animation overlay. No text embedded in the artwork. No dashboard UI, no realistic banking screens, no enterprise style.
```

## Alternate Prompt

```text
Create a cute startup splash scene for MichiFinanzas with Tikki, Ludo, and Manchas gathered around a playful savings mission. The setting should feel cozy and magical, with yarn balls, coins, and tiny savings props that suggest progress, habit-building, and money wins. Use soft gradients, premium illustration polish, bright teal and cream tones, warm gold highlights, and a clean central composition for mobile. Keep it charming, optimistic, and high quality, with clear negative space for a loading indicator.
```

## Negative Constraints

- Do not add text into the art.
- Do not make the background busy or dark.
- Do not use realistic UI screens as the main focus.
- Do not let one mascot dominate the splash entirely.
- Do not make it look like a children’s book cover; it should still feel premium.

## Export Notes

- Portrait-first mobile composition.
- Keep central safe area clean for logo/loading overlay.
- Bright enough to feel welcoming, but with enough contrast for foreground elements.
