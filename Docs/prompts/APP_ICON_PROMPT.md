# SpendSage App Icon Prompt

Last updated: 2026-04-05

## Recommendation

For the iOS app icon, use a single mascot, not the trio. At small sizes, one character reads better and feels more premium.

Required lead:
- `Manchas` only

Recommended composition:
- Manchas peeking over a soft ledge, ribbon, or rounded frame edge
- head and paws large enough to stay readable at tiny icon sizes
- background with cute premium atmosphere, not empty and not noisy
- one clear ledge only, with no bars, cut blocks, or strip-like overlays crossing the face
- face clearly above the ledge, with both eyes readable and the silhouette dominated by the head and ears

## Primary Prompt

```text
Design a premium iOS app icon for SpendSage using only Manchas, the black-and-white mascot cat. Manchas should appear in a peeking pose inspired by a cat looking over a line or ledge, with the face and paws reading immediately at tiny sizes. Make Manchas much larger than in the current icon and keep the character faithful to the existing SpendSage art style: rounded chibi proportions, glossy eyes, clean linework, soft blush, green bow, and polished shading. The background should feel cute, premium, and memorable with teal, mint, cream, and warm gold accents, soft glow, rounded shapes, and subtle decorative details. No other mascots, no text, no dashboard panels, no clutter, and no object that competes with Manchas as the focal point. Compose it as a clean square iOS icon with safe margins and strong silhouette clarity.
```

## Alternate Prompt

```text
Design a premium iOS app icon for SpendSage with Manchas as the only visible mascot. Use a playful peeking composition where Manchas rises over a soft ledge with paws visible and the face occupying most of the icon. Keep the art highly detailed but still clean and readable for App Store scale. Use a cute premium background with airy gradients, subtle sparkles, rounded framing, and gentle teal, mint, cream, and soft gold lighting. The result should feel warm, expensive, and unmistakably SpendSage, not generic, childish, or busy. No text, no extra mascots, no heavy finance props, and no miniature dashboard UI.
```

## Negative Constraints

- Do not use Tikki or Ludo in the icon.
- Do not use all three mascots in the icon.
- Do not include text or logo wordmark.
- Do not add dense finance UI, charts, or a big money object that steals attention.
- Do not use flat corporate banking style.
- Do not let background decor overpower Manchas.
- Do not place any horizontal bar, hard crop block, or thick strip across Manchas.
- Do not hide the eyes or compress the face below the ledge.
- Do not make the lower ledge heavy enough to feel like a second focal point.

## Export Notes

- Square master, high resolution.
- Leave safe margins for iOS icon masking.
- Prioritize face readability, paws, and silhouette clarity over extra decoration.

## Xcode 26 Variant Notes

- Deliver three masters into `AppIcon.appiconset`: `Any`, `Dark`, and `Tinted`.
- `Any`: bright premium default icon.
- `Dark`: deeper background and warmer highlights so the icon still reads in dark home screen styles.
- `Tinted`: simplified high-contrast version with fewer internal colors and a stronger silhouette.
